Flappy Rogue - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Package ID: www.flappyrogue.app

This Privacy Policy applies to the Flappy Rogue mobile application.

English

1. Data We Process

  • In-game progression data (such as high score, gold/coins collected, character unlock status, and purchased meta-upgrades).
  • App configurations (such as sound effects, music toggles, and color theme preferences).

2. Where Data Is Stored

All progression, settings, and high score data are stored locally on your device. The app does not transmit your data to any external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Flappy Rogue operates entirely offline and does not integrate any third-party payment systems, analytics platforms, or ad networks.

4. Children

Since we do not collect, store, or share any personal information, the app is completely safe for children.

5. Your Choices

  • You can reset or remove all in-game progress by clearing the app data through your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

7. Contact

For questions regarding this policy, please reach out via the developer contact info on the Google Play listing.
